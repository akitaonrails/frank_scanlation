# FRANK Scanlation v0.3.0

## Reading protections

Fansub-site ads that hijack your reading are now countered at three
layers, all site-agnostic and deliberately minimal:

- **Navigation jail**: while a manga is open, the app refuses any
  navigation that leaves the manga's own domains — ad redirects and
  popunders die at the navigation layer and the page stays put.
  Subdomain mirrors of the same site remain allowed.
- **Popup kill**: `window.open` is disabled on reading pages, before
  any ad script gets a chance to grab it.
- **Overlay buster**: freshly-injected full-screen, high-z-index
  overlay ads (transparent click-catchers, fullscreen ad iframes) are
  hidden on sight. Site chrome present at load is never touched.

## Fix: sites that migrate mirror subdomains

`w4.example.com` → `w6.example.com` style migrations no longer break
chapter extraction, new-chapter checks, or progress tracking — "same
site" now means "same apex domain" everywhere.
